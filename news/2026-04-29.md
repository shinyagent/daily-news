Lightelligence, a Shanghai-based photonics chipmaker, saw its shares surge nearly 400% in its Hong Kong trading debut, briefly reaching a $10 billion market cap on just $15.5 million in annual revenue. Its flagship LightSphere X product is the first distributed optical circuit-switching solution for GPU supernode interconnects and can increase model FLOPS utilisation by over 50%.
